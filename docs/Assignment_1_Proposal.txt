1 Music Map Explorer:
Create an interactive map app that allows users to explore the world through music. 
Users can click on different locations on the map to discover music that originates from or is popular in that region.

2 Soundtrack Composer for Life Events:
Design an app that helps users create soundtracks for significant life events, such as weddings, birthdays, or vacations. 
Users can select songs from Spotify or Apple Music to match the emotions and memories associated with each event.
